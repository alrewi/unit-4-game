Crystal Power

You'll be given a random number at the beginning of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score surpasses the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game restarts, the value of each crystal changes.
